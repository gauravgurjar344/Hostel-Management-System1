About


AARYABHATT is one of the Chitkara University's male hostel.It promises a well-maintained, aesthetic, and comfortable accomodation to its residents.We boast about being the largest- capacity hostal in campus as it is a seven story building and houses around 680 inhabitants from various academic streams such as B.Tech, PhD, etc.The hostel features a fully functional mess, an air-conditioned reading room, a gym, a beauty parlor, a laundary room and a common room. Aside from this, other cultural activities, such as DJ nights and Movie nights, are held annually for the residents. We also have a thriving sports culture with passionate participation in sporting events throughout the year. We have 24x7 CCTV surveilliance of the hostel common areas for student's safety and security.



Vision


Infusing a collective identity in AARYABHATT hostel residents and ensuring that each individual is cared about and provided for.
